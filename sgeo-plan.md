# SEO and GEO Strategy for Web Presence (TheDroneEdge.com)

This plan outlines a strategy to improve web presence through traditional Search Engine Optimization (SEO) and Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)—positioning content for citation by AI models/agents.

## 1\. Goals Breakdown

| Category                | Short-Term Goals (0-3 Months)                                                                                                                                                                                 | Long-Term Goals (6+ Months)                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| :---------------------: | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | :-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Foundation (SEO)**    | Improve website technical health (speed, mobile-friendliness, fix broken links). Implement a clear, simple URL structure. Ensure all core content is unique, easy-to-read, and has a clear heading structure. | Build domain and topical authority by consistently publishing high-quality, in-depth, comprehensive content. Earn high-trust backlinks from authoritative industry sources. Maintain up-to-date and reliable information.               |
| **AI Visibility (GEO)** | Restructure existing content to be easily extractable: "front-load" sections with direct answers, use short paragraphs, and utilize numbered/bulleted lists. Define technical terms immediately for clarity.  | Achieve high citation frequency and "Share of Voice" in AI responses. Establish strong entity clarity (consistent brand/product names) across your site and third-party platforms. Drive branded searches by being a recognized source. |

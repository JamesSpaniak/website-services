────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
TITLE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Drone delivery scales from demos to limited commercial routes (Amazon, Walmart partners, Zipline, Wing)

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SUMMARY
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Major retailers and logistics firms moved drone delivery from stunt videos to constrained real operations in select U.S. and international locales—often under tight FAA waivers/partnerships or country-specific frameworks. Medical deliveries in Africa and Asia remained an early success story alongside suburban retail experiments.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
KEY INSIGHTS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
- Economics hinge on route density, weather, noise politics, and crash risk tolerance.
- Regulatory BVLOS progress remains the bottleneck in many jurisdictions.
- Public acceptance is hyper-local: one town’s approval isn’t another’s.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
TOPIC
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Logistics; retail; healthcare supply chain; urban air mobility (lower altitude)

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
GENRE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Business / technology

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
MOOD
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Cautiously optimistic; occasionally skeptical

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PUBLIC OPINION
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Excitement about convenience and speed competes with noise complaints and privacy fears (cameras, flight paths). Medical drone stories generally drew warmer coverage than retail buzz over suburban rooftops.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
FOLLOW-UPS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
BVLOS rulemakings; operator consolidation; incidents driving local moratoria; integration with weather modeling and detect-and-avoid research.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
TIMELINE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Steady 2021–2026 progression with uneven geography—some markets leap ahead, others stall.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SIGNIFICANCE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Demonstrates that the hardest part of drone delivery is societal and regulatory, not just engineering.

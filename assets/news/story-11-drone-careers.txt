SECTOR MAP (covered in JSON prose — expand in 11a/11b/11c later)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

AGRICULTURE & PRECISION AG
- Roles: crop scouting pilot, agronomist-facing data analyst, spray/dispense (where legal), program manager.
- Deliverables: NDVI/multispectral, stand counts, irrigation stress, variable-rate support.
- Path: Part 107 → photogrammetry platforms → seasonal → ag-tech integrator.

FIRE: IMAGING, PREVENTION, SUPPRESSION SUPPORT
- Prevention vs incident vs R&D; MSO/vendor; NWCG-style quals; thermal + ICS.

DELIVERY & LOGISTICS
- BVLOS ops, route safety, maintenance, UTM; medical-first routes; link story-06, advance-03.

PHOTOGRAPHY, FILM, REAL ESTATE
- Saturated entry; pivot to inspection or licensed corporate work.

INFRASTRUCTURE & ENERGY INSPECTION
- Lines, turbines, solar thermal; LiDAR corridors; higher pay than creative.

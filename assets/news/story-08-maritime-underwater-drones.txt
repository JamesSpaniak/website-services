────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
TITLE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Maritime and underwater drone systems move to center stage (Black Sea, Pacific, mine warfare)

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SUMMARY
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Uncrewed surface vessels (USVs) and underwater vehicles featured prominently in naval strategy reporting—mine detection, harbor surveillance, and long-range strikes using maritime drones. The Black Sea conflict provided high-profile examples of USV attacks and evolving counter-tactics.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
KEY INSIGHTS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
- Surface drones can punch above their weight against ships if ISR and targeting chains work.
- Underwater autonomy remains hard: navigation, communication, endurance.
- Civilian tech (starlink-like links, commercial cameras) again blurred into naval warfare.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
TOPIC
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Naval warfare; mine countermeasures; ISR; autonomous vessels

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
GENRE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

MOOD

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PUBLIC OPINION
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
General audiences discovered USVs through viral attack videos; expert commentary emphasized escalation risks in chokepoints. Environmental groups raised questions about fuel spills and debris from maritime drone engagements.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
FOLLOW-UPS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Naval doctrine updates; harbor defenses; electronic attack against drone boat datalinks; allied exercises integrating USVs.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
TIMELINE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Notable public episodes across 2022–2026 with accelerating procurement interest worldwide.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SIGNIFICANCE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
TITLE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Counter-UAS (C-UAS) becomes a major industry: airports, stadiums, and battlefields (2021–2026)

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SUMMARY
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
As drone threats diversified (reckless hobbyists, criminal smuggling, and state-sponsored systems), demand grew for detection (radar, RF, EO/IR) and defeat (jamming, spoofing, nets, directed energy). Airports and large events expanded mitigation programs; militaries layered C-UAS into layered air defense.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
KEY INSIGHTS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
- Legal constraints on jamming remain thorny in civilian airspace (collateral communications impacts).
- False positives and operator training are operational pain points.
- C-UAS procurement often lags fast-evolving drone threats.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
TOPIC
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Airport security; spectrum policy; defense procurement; public safety

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
GENRE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Industrial / homeland security

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
MOOD
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Security-forward; occasionally skeptical of vendor hype

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
PUBLIC OPINION
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Air travelers generally supported protections after high-profile near-miss reporting. Civil liberties groups cautioned about surveillance creep from wide-area drone monitoring. Military audiences focused on cost-exchange against cheap drones.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
FOLLOW-UPS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Integration with ATC systems; standards for airport C-UAS; battlefield C-UAS paired with EW; insurance and liability frameworks.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
TIMELINE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Steady buildout across the 2020s with recurring incident-driven funding spikes.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
SIGNIFICANCE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Created a whole parallel market: stopping drones as big a business as flying them.
